Question 1: A sequence of numbers is given as 2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ___. What is the next number in the sequence?
A) 40
B) 42
C) 46
D) 56

The answer is:42
2 + 4 = 6
6 + 6 = 12
12 + 8 = 20
20 + 10 = 30
The increment is increasing by 2 each time: Next increment: 30 + 12 = 42

Question 2: A car travels 150 kilometers in 3 hours. What is its average speed in kilometers per hour?
A) 45 km/h
B) 50 km/h
C) 55 km/h
D) 60 km/h

The answer is:50km/h
Average speed is calculated as: Average speed=Total distance/Total time
So, Average speed=150 km/3 hours=50 km/h

Question 5:If today is Wednesday, what day of the week will it be 10 days from today?

The answer is:Saturday
To determine the day of the week 10 days from today (Wednesday), we can use the concept of modulo arithmetic. There are 7 days in a week, so we find the remainder when 10 is divided by 7.
10√∑7=1 remainder 3 This means 10 days from today is 3 days after Wednesday. Thus, if today is Wednesday, 10 days from today will be:
Wednesday+3 days=Saturday

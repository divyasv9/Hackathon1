Verbal Interview Tips

Strong verbal communication skills are essential for interviews. Here are some tips and practice questions to help you prepare:

  • Speak clearly and concisely.
  • Use strong vocabulary and grammar.
  • Actively listen and respond to questions.
  • Practice common interview questions like "Tell me about yourself" or "Why do you want this job?"

Practice Questions for Placement exams

Question 1: Cat is to Kitten as Dog is to ______.

The answer is: Puppy

Question 2:Choose the word that is most similar in meaning to "Happy".

The answer is: Joyful

Question 3:Choose the word that is most opposite in meaning to "Increase".

The answer is: Reduce

Question 4:He was known for his ______ and never turned away anyone in need.
A) generosity
B) hostility
C) arrogance
D) indifference

The answer is: A) generosity

Question 5:All roses are flowers. Some flowers fade quickly. Therefore, ______.
A) All roses fade quickly
B) Some roses fade quickly
C) No roses fade quickly
D) Some flowers do not fade quickly

The answer is: B)Some roses fade quickly

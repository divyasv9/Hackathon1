VLSI Design

Common VLSI Design Questions

Question 1: Which type of VLSI design style combines the speed of custom design with the flexibility of standard-cell design?

The answer is: Semi-Custom

Question 2:What does CMOS stand for in VLSI design?
A) Complementary Metal-Oxide Semiconductor
B) Complex Metal-Oxide Semiconductor
C) Complementary Metal-Oxide System
D) Complex Metal-Oxide System

The answer is: A) Complementary Metal-Oxide Semiconductor

Question 3:In VLSI, what is the primary purpose of using a clock tree?
A) To distribute power evenly across the chip
B) To synchronize the timing of various components
C) To enhance signal integrity
D) To reduce the overall power consumption

The answer is: B) To synchronize the timing of various components

Question 4:Which of the following is a key advantage of using FinFET technology over traditional planar MOSFETs?
A) Lower cost
B) Higher speed
C) Better control over short-channel effects
D) Simpler fabrication process

The answer is: C) Better control over short-channel effects

Question 5:In VLSI design, what is meant by the term 'Design for Testability' (DFT)?
A) Designing circuits that are easy to manufacture
B) Designing circuits that consume less power
C) Designing circuits that are easy to test and debug
D) Designing circuits with higher performance

The answer is: C) Designing circuits that are easy to test and debug
